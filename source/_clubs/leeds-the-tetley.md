description: |-
  Welcome to our fortnightly Blood on the Clocktower event!
  We usually have 3 simultaneous games going: beginner, intermediate, and advanced.
  For the beginner games, there will be a full rules explanation, but if you've played other social deduction games, like Werewolf or Town of Salem, it will work in a similar way. This games will be the Trouble Brewing script, which is the introductory one. You can find videos on Youtube if you want to see how it works, just search for Trouble Brewing.
  For the intermediate games we often play one of the more advanced base scripts, either Bad Moon Rising or Sects and Violets, but some custom scripts can also be included in the mix. These have some extra mechanics compared to Trouble Brewing, so an explanation of what to look out for will be included.
  For the advanced players, anything goes! We do still play some of the base scripts, but this game will include more custom scripts, from the Carousel and from players. There will be a lot more going on in these games compared to the intermediate games, but rules clarifications will still be available.
  Our first game usually begins around 18:00, so arrive by then if you want to make sure you're counted in, but there are options to join in part way through a game if you arrive later.
