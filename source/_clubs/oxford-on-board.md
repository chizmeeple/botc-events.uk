description: |-
  In the quiet town of Ravenswood Bluff, ‌a demon walks amongst you...
  Blood on the Clocktower is a group social deduction game (think much improved Werewolves/Mafia). The good townsfolk must put the puzzle together in time to execute the secret demon of the group who, along with their minions, must lie and bluff their way to victory... by killing all the good players!
  The twists with Blood on the Clocktower are:
  Everyone has a unique power
  &
  The dead play on...
  . . .
  New to the game? Don't worry, we'll explain the rules. For an example of the game try:
  https://youtu.be/m14N28Lq-jM
  Running late? Not a problem, there's a character role for this where you can join late or leave early, best to give us a heads up.
  . . .
