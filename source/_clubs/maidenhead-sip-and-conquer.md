  Blood on the Clocktower is back, and our storytellers are more devious than ever!
  ON THE STROKE OF MIDNIGHT...During a hellish thunderstorm, a scream echoes through the sleepy town of Ravenswood Bluff. The townsfolk rush to investigate and find the beloved local storyteller has been murdered, their body hanging limp from the clocktower. As blood drips onto the cobblestones below, a realisation slowly dawns… a demon has been unleashed, killing by night and taking on human form by day. Can good find the demon in time? Or will evil overrun this once peaceful town?
  Join us to play Blood on the Clocktower, where we'll have two devious storytellers hosting simultaneous games for our courageous players. Tickets include a cocktail or mocktail to get you into the Ravenswood Bluff atmosphere! Open to experienced and beginners villagers alike. If there are enough folks who have played before, our storytellers may run a more advanced game.
