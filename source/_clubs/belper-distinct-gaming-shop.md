description: >-
  The beloved storyteller of Belper has been found murdered and dangling from
  the local clocktower. The residents have been called to find who is evil
  enough to have performed this deed.

  This session will be running a mixed script, with some new additions from The
  Carousel expansion and is suitable for experienced players who have played at
  least 5 sessions previously and are confident with a mixture of roles.

  Blood on the Clocktower is a social deduction game where players on the Good
  team try to find and execute the Demon, while the Evil team deceives them to
  stay hidden. Guided by a Storyteller, players use secret abilities at Night
  and discuss suspicions by Day, voting on whom to execute. Unlike other games,
  eliminated players stay involved, adding to the strategy and intrigue until
  the end.

  Entry is £5 per person and is first come, first served, and limited to
  maximum 20 players, so please contact the shop to request your place. Payment
  in advance will secure it.
